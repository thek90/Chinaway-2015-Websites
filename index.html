About Us

ChinaWay has led American student volunteers to conduct public benefits work for four consecutive years in bases of China Giant Panda Protection And Research Center. Besides showing love and care for those lovely pandas, we also interact with lovable kids in the nearby countryside schools. Little by little and step by step, the teaching methods such as practical English and practical software application are gradually recognized and loved by kids.

Inspired by this, "GeLiVable Fund" and Wolong Panda Volunteer Service Center plan to explore alongside the way from the Wolong Panda base to Baoxing Panda base so as to form a “Panda Public Benefits Circle". At the meantime, the two will work together with foreign public benefits organizations to initiate activities centering around the "Panda Public Benefits Circle".

"Panda Public Benefits Circle" 2014 Chinese and American Volunteers Special Activity has officially opened up. American middlle school students has begun to do public benefits work within the circle. Those lovable, kind and earnest students come a far way from America to western China.They devote themselves to public benefts work and are greatly appreciated. Their spirit to pursue excellence is worth lerning from. Be it Chinese volunteers or foreign ones, they all can realize their public benefits dreams by joining us.

Love, serving as a bridge between people, will make limitless contribution. May the tiny contribution at the very beginning will eventually spread to the whole world.

关于我们

ChinaWay已经连续四年带领美国的学生志愿者团来中国保护大熊猫研究中心各基地展开相关公益活动。除了体现对熊猫的关爱以外，也与周边乡村学校的孩子们进行互动。无形中，由中西方学生创造出的实践英语、实践软件应用等教学的模式已深入人心，孩子们格外喜爱。

受此启发，“给力基金”与卧龙熊猫志愿者服务中心计划将卧龙与宝兴的大熊猫科普野外探险之路打通，打造“大熊猫公益圈”，并联合各国公益机构全面发起“大熊猫公益圈”互助互爱公益行动。

“大熊猫公益圈”2014中美志愿者专题活动正式拉开帷幕。来自美国的中学生志愿者开始在这个圈内展开各项公益行动。这帮活泼可爱、优秀善良的孩子们，不远万里来到中国西部，从事公益，不愧为当代美国版的活雷锋。他们的这种精益求精的精神是值得我们学习的。从此岸到彼岸，从彼岸到此岸。无论东方还是西方，一批批志愿者，都将在这条道路上，实现自己的公益梦想。

奉献爱心犹如修路铺桥，功德无限。愿这种功德，能像星星之火一样，可以燎原。
